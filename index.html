Who we are

A dedicated web team for growing businesses

Digital Squad is a web development team focused on helping businesses establish and grow their online presence. Before writing a single line of code, we take the time to understand your goals, your audience, and your brand.

We don't do templates or one-size-fits-all packages. Every project is a unique challenge that deserves a tailored solution. We communicate openly, work transparently, and build websites that genuinely serve your business.

How it works

Our Process

Simple, transparent, and collaborative. No jargon, no surprises — just a clear path from idea to live website.

1

Discuss your idea

We start with a conversation. You tell us about your business, your goals, and what you need from your website. No commitment required — just a genuine exchange of ideas.

2

Plan the structure

Together we map out the pages, layout, and content your site will need. We align on scope, timeline, and pricing before any work begins — fully transparent.

3

Design & development

We design and build your website with care. You're involved throughout — we share progress, gather your feedback, and refine until it's exactly right.

4

Delivery & support

Once you're happy, we launch. We walk you through how to use and manage your site, and we're available for questions and ongoing support after delivery.
